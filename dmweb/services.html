Consulting Services

We offer specialized consulting services in strategic mine planning and economic evaluation of mining projects.

We have worked with several projects and ongoing mining operations, addressing pressing specific issues, or helping mining companies evaluate their current strategy and find new opportunities and insights. Usually using DeepMine as a powerful tool to generate scenarios and data for in-depth analysis.

Custom Development

BOAMine has developed unique and in depth knowledge of the problem of strategic mine planning of open pit mines, as well as an advance set of tools and algorithms, some of which is accessible to the industry through DeepMine.

But we know that each mine is unique and faces particular challenges. Some of which can be very difficult to surmount without proper optimization methodologies. For those rare and hard to solve problems, we offer specialized software developments.

Technical Training

We are committed to placing strategy in the center stage of mine planning. As part of this effort, we offer in-depth technical training in strategic mine planning for open pit mines, with a strong focus on building a robust foundation in fundamental and advanced theoretical concepts.
